## Executive Overview
This repository contains the end-to-end analytical pipeline for the **CARB-X x Gates Foundation Strategic Initiative**. By synthesizing 16 WHO global datasets, this project moves beyond "one-size-fits-all" public health strategies to deliver a **Precision Intervention Matrix** for the world's 30 High-Burden Countries (HBCs).

The objective is to identify exactly which biological, clinical, and structural barriers prevent TB eradication in specific contexts and to prescribe evidence-based solutions—ranging from **Pharmacy-led AI Triage** to **Nutrition-Dx Bundles**.

---

##  The 9-Stage Analytical Pipeline

### Phase 1: Foundation & Market Intelligence
* **01_Data_Audit:** Comprehensive quality and completeness check of 16 WHO datasets.
* **02_Feature_Engineering:** Construction of the **CARB-X Index**, a composite metric for health system resilience.
* **03_Market_Segmentation:** K-Means clustering to identify 4 distinct "Market Archetypes" for diagnostic deployment.

### Phase 2: AI Readiness & Predictive Modeling
* **04_Gates_AI_Readiness:** A 2x2 matrix analysis of technical infrastructure vs. clinical burden.
* **05_2030_Trajectory:** Comparative forecasting of "Business as Usual" vs. "CARB-X Accelerated" outcomes.

### Phase 3: Technical & Biological Deep Dives
* **06_Computer_Vision:** Evaluation of AI diagnostic limits (ResNet50/YOLOv8) across different sputum qualities.
* **07_Drug_Resistance:** Mapping MDR/XDR-TB gaps and the structural capacity for Drug Susceptibility Testing (DST).
* **08_Equity_&_Prevention:** Identifying the "Left Behind"—focusing on gender gaps, TPT failure, and HIV co-infection.

### Phase 4: Strategic Synthesis
* **09_Precision_Intervention:** The final "Master Pipeline" that converts all findings into a country-level prescription matrix.

##  Key Deliverables

### 1. The Precision Intervention Matrix
The primary output is a risk-adjusted roadmap for the 30 High-Burden Countries.
* **Mozambique (Priority #1):** Highlights high vulnerability (Score: 60.0) requiring a dual focus on **Nutrition-Dx** and **Pharmacy AI Triage**.
* **Tactical Labels:** Every country is assigned a specific "Prescription" (e.g., Digital TPT Trackers, Emergency Grants, or Dual Molecular Dx).



### 2. Fiscal Accountability Audit
A critical assessment of UNHLM commitments versus actual domestic funding.
* **Sustainability Risk:** Identifies countries with <30% domestic funding where the Gates Foundation must act as a "Strategic Co-investor" rather than a passive donor.

### 3. Socio-Biological Risk Mapping
A visualization of how non-clinical factors (undernutrition, gender-based health-seeking behavior) dictate the success of medical technology.

##  Installation & Usage
1.  **Environment:** Designed for Python 3.12+ (Jupyter/Anaconda).
2.  **Dependencies:** `pandas`, `numpy`, `matplotlib`, `seaborn`, `sklearn`.
3.  **Data:** Requires the latest WHO Global TB Report CSVs (Burden, Budget, TPT, Strategy, etc.).
4.  **Execution:** Notebooks are sequential. Data generated in Notebook 01-08 is required for the final synthesis in Notebook 09.
